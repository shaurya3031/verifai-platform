About Verif-AI

Verif-AI was built to solve a critical problem in the modern information ecosystem: anyone can publish anything, and it's increasingly hard to know what's true.

We combine the power of NVIDIA's cutting-edge AI models with real-time Google Search corroboration to give you a multi-dimensional, reliable verdict on any news claim — in seconds.

Our mission is to put professional-grade fact-checking tools in the hands of everyone: journalists, researchers, students, and everyday readers who care about truth.

Privacy Policy
